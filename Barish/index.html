In India, the monsoon is not weather. It is an event. The entire country waits for it. And when it arrives, everything changes in an hour.

You have your own monsoons. The conversation that broke the tension. The cry that came in the bathroom at school when no one was looking. The moment of forgiveness that washed months of anger away.

Rain is not always gentle. Sometimes it floods. But even floods recede, and the soil they leave behind is the richest kind. Name the rains in your life. They cleansed more than you knew at the time.
