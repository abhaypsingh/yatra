Silsilā

A silsila is a chain — an unbroken line connecting one moment to the next, one person to another, across years and distances.

You are full of borrowed things. The way you laugh might be your father’s. The way you stir your tea might be your grandmother’s. These are not coincidences. They are silsilas.

Trace the threads. Every chain you find is proof that you are not alone in time.
