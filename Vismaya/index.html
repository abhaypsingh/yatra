Vismaya

Vismaya is the Sanskrit word for wonder — that sharp inhale when something catches you off guard with its beauty or strangeness.

A spider’s web jeweled with dew. The way a flock of birds turns all at once. Light doing something unexpected with water. The first star appearing while the sky is still blue.

Most people stop noticing these things as they grow older. This is your way of not letting that happen.
