the call

Sometimes something calls to you — a subject that fascinates you, a cause that angers you, a person who needs you. You cannot always explain it. The call is not always loud. Sometimes it is a quiet pull, a persistent thought, a dream that returns. Name what is calling you.

A calling is different from an ambition. Ambition is what you decide. A calling is what decides you.

It shows up as a restlessness — why does this injustice bother me so much? Why can I not stop thinking about the sea? Why do I come alive when I teach a younger child?

You do not have to answer every call today. But you should name them. Because ignoring a calling does not make it quiet. It just makes it louder.
