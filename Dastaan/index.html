Dāstān

A saga is not just a story. It is a commandment disguised as a bedtime tale.

When your grandmother tells you about her father’s sacrifice, she is not only remembering — she is telling you what she expects of you. When the family legend is about resilience, you learn that complaining is not welcome here.

These sagas make you brave. They also make you tired. Name the family stories that shaped you. Then ask: am I living my own saga, or repeating theirs?
