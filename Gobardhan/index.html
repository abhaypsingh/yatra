Gobardhan

The city person sees dung and looks away. The village woman sees fuel, insulation, building material, and sacred offering. On Govardhan Puja, the dung cake is worshipped — because it kept the family warm, it cooked their food, it was the difference between surviving winter and not. What is humble is not what lacks value — it is what is undervalued by those who do not know its use.

You have made gobardhans. The skills others dismissed that became your livelihood. The experiences that seemed like waste but became the fuel for your next chapter. The humble beginnings that built a mountain.
