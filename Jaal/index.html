Jāl

The same word — jaal — means both a safety net and a trap. That is not a coincidence. The things that protect you can also confine you.

Your family is a net. It catches you when you fall, but it can also prevent you from flying. Your friend group is a net. It connects you, but it can also trap you in a version of yourself you have outgrown.

The internet itself is a jaal — connecting you to the world while catching you in loops of scrolling, comparing, and performing. Name the nets. Know which threads to keep and which to cut.
