The cosmic accounts are kept in a ledger no one can read. You give without expecting return, you suffer without expecting compensation, and then — years later, in a form you never predicted — something arrives that settles a debt you had forgotten was owed. Karmic returns do not come in the currency you expect. The kindness you showed a stranger returns as a stranger’s kindness to your child.

You have experienced ugaahi. The unexpected repayment for years of patience. The reward that arrived long after you stopped waiting. The cosmic settling of accounts that you had written off as losses.

Name the collections. They are the proof that the ledger, however mysterious, is not empty.
