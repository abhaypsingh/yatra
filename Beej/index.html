A seed does not look like what it will become. It is small, dark, unremarkable. But inside it is a blueprint for something enormous — a tree, a flower, a forest.

You carry seeds too. Ideas that were planted years ago by a conversation, a book, a moment of wonder. They sit in the soil of you, waiting for the right season. Some will bloom soon. Some will take years. Some may never grow — but they are still part of your inner landscape.

Name the seeds you carry. Even the ones you cannot yet imagine blooming.
