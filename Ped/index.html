A tree does not rush. It does not compare its height to the trees around it. It simply grows — one ring at a time, one season at a time.

The things that matter most in you are growing the same way. Your understanding of right and wrong. Your ability to love without losing yourself. Your capacity to be alone without being lonely.

These trees need time. They need rain and sun and sometimes storms. Name what is growing. Trust the pace. The roots matter more than the height.
