Maṭkā

In a world of refrigerators and water purifiers, the matka seems old-fashioned. But nothing tastes like matka water on a hot day. The coolness comes not from machinery but from the earth itself — porous, patient, generous.

The best things in your life might be matkas. Simple. Unpolished. Made of common material. But they hold what matters and keep it cool when everything else is burning. A grandmother’s advice. A friend who listens. A routine that steadies you.

Name the matkas. They are easily broken and impossible to replace.
