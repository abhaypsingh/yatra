The story goes that Lord Ram was so moved by the squirrel’s effort — tiny pebbles placed with enormous love — that he stroked its back and left three lines that squirrels carry to this day.

The world tells you that only big things matter. But the truth is that most of what holds life together is small. A thank-you note. An extra minute of listening. Showing up when you did not have to. These pebbles build bridges.

Name your small efforts. They are not small at all.
