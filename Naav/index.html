Every life has rivers — changes you did not choose and changes you did. Some crossings are smooth. Some capsize you.

Moving cities. Losing a friend. Watching your parents change. Choosing a stream in school that closes other doors.

The boat is not always sturdy and the water is not always calm. But here is the truth about crossings: you only need to keep rowing. You do not need to see the other shore to reach it.
