Pṛthivī पृथ्वी

Pṛthivī means earth, the ground beneath your feet. In Indian cosmology, it is one of the five great elements — solid, patient, enduring. It holds everything without complaint.

Every natural phenomenon contains a lesson about how to live. Storms teach about emotional turbulence. Rivers teach about persistence. Seeds teach about faith in darkness. These are not metaphors imposed on nature — they are wisdom nature has been demonstrating for eons.
