Sapna

In Hindi, sapna means “dream” — both the sleeping kind and the waking kind. This space is for the first.

Dreams are strange, beautiful, sometimes frightening messengers. They remix your day, your fears, your hopes into stories only you can tell.

Write them down before they fade. Over time, you may notice patterns — recurring places, feelings, people. Your dreaming mind knows things your waking mind forgets.

Your dreams are saved only on this device.
