Pardā

A curtain is not a wall. A wall blocks everything. A curtain can be drawn open or closed. You have that choice — even if it does not always feel like it.

Some curtains you draw to survive — hiding your tears from a younger sibling who needs you to be strong. Some you draw out of habit — the filter between who you are and who you present online.

There is no shame in having curtains. The question is whether you still remember what is behind them. Name the curtains. Not to tear them down, but to know where the real you is standing.
