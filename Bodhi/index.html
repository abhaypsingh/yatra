Bodhi

Bodhi is the Sanskrit word for awakening — the moment the Buddha understood the nature of suffering under the Bodhi tree.

Your awakenings don’t have to be that grand. Maybe you suddenly understood why your friend was upset. Maybe fractions finally made sense. Maybe you realized something about yourself that you couldn’t see before.

These small moments of clarity are how you grow. Write them down so you never forget that you are always learning.
