Jugnu

The firefly — jugnu — is a tiny creature that carries its own light. In Hindi, calling someone “jugnu” means they bring brightness wherever they go.

This jar holds your gratitudes. Each one is small — maybe just a sentence — but together they glow.

On hard days, come back and watch your fireflies. You have always had light inside you.

Your fireflies are saved only on this device.
