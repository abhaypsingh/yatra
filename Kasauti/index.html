the touchstone

A touchstone reveals what is real gold and what is fake. Life has touchstones too — moments that test what you are truly made of. Not exams. Not marks. The moment a friend needed you and you had to choose between convenience and loyalty. The time honesty was expensive and you had to decide if you could afford it. Kasauti asks you to name your real tests.

Kasauṭī

The tests that matter have no syllabus and no marks. They arrive without warning and demand an answer right now.

Do you speak up when the group is bullying someone? Do you tell the truth when lying is easier? Do you show up for someone when showing up costs you something?

You have already taken some of these tests. You passed some. You failed some. Both are valuable. The failures taught you what to work on. The passes taught you what you are made of. Name them. They are your real report card.
