We celebrate fullness and fear emptiness. But the moon teaches a different lesson.

A crescent is not a broken full moon. It is its own kind of beautiful. The new moon — invisible, dark — is not gone. It is resting.

You will have weeks where you feel full of energy, ideas, and confidence. And weeks where you feel like you are barely there. Both are phases. Both pass. Name them so you can see the pattern — and trust that after every dark, the light returns.
