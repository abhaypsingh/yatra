Vana वन

Vana (Sanskrit: forest, grove) represents the wild, living, growing part of nature. In the Indian tradition, the forest is not wilderness to be conquered but a sacred space of growth, reflection, and transformation.

Your forest grows from real actions. Each tree is something you actually did — a kindness shown, a truth spoken, a patience held, a courage found, a gratitude felt. Over time, these small acts become a living forest.

You cannot see yourself growing. But your forest can show you. Come back each day. Plant something real. Watch what grows.
