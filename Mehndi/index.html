Mehndi

Mehndi teaches you that beauty takes patience. You cannot rush it. You sit still while it is applied. You wait while it dries. You resist the urge to scratch. And slowly, over hours, the green paste flakes away and underneath is the most beautiful red — a colour that was always there but needed time to emerge.

There is a saying: the darker the mehndi, the deeper the love. This is true beyond henna. The things that colour you most deeply are the ones you sat with longest. The friendships that survived years. The truths that you resisted but slowly absorbed.
