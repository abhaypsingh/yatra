Chitta

Chitta is the Sanskrit word for consciousness — the canvas on which all your thoughts, moods, and feelings appear.

This is the simplest experience in the entire journey. One color. One word. No explanation needed. Over days and weeks, your tiles build into something beautiful — an abstract painting of your inner weather.

There are no wrong colors and no wrong words. Whatever you feel is real, and it belongs here.
