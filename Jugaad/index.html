Jugaad is an Indian word that has no exact English translation. It means finding a way when there is no way. Making something work with whatever you have. It is the opposite of giving up.

The best solutions are often not the expensive ones. They are the clever ones. The paper clip that fixed the glasses. The shortcut through the park. The way you explained something complicated using only simple words.
