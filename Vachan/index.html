Vachan

A vachan is sacred. In the old traditions, a promise spoken aloud becomes real — it lives in the air between people.

Not all promises can be kept. But the act of making them matters. It says: this is who I want to be.

Write your promises down. Let them remind you of the person you are becoming.

Your promises are stored only on this device.
