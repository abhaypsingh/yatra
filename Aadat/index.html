A habit is an action that has become invisible. You do it without deciding, without noticing, without effort. That is both the power and the danger of habits.

The habit of reading before bed builds your mind while you rest. The habit of checking your phone every three minutes fragments your attention. You did not choose most of your habits. They chose you. But you can choose which ones to keep.

Name your habits. The invisible architects of your daily life. Which ones are building the person you want to be?
