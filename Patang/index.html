A kite without a string is just paper in the wind. It tumbles, directionless, free in name but lost in truth. The string is not a chain — it is the connection that gives the kite its shape in the sky.

The best kites fly highest when the string is taut, not loose. The same is true for you. The people who hold your string — parents, teachers, friends — are not holding you back. They are holding you up.

But sometimes you need more string. And sometimes they need to let out a little more. Name the connections that keep you flying.
