About Rangoli

Rangoli is the ancient art of drawing symmetric patterns at the threshold of a home — a way of welcoming beauty into everyday life.

Here, every stroke you make is mirrored into a mandala. There is no wrong line. Just draw, and let the pattern emerge.

Part of Yatra — a journey of 56 experiences.
