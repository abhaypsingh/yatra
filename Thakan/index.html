The world admires people who never stop. But stopping is not failure. Tiredness is information. It tells you where you are spending too much, where you are pretending, where you need help.

There is a tiredness that sleep cannot fix — the tiredness of carrying too many expectations, of performing a version of yourself that does not fit, of giving more than you have. This tiredness asks not for rest, but for honesty.

Name your tiredness. What it is really about. The naming itself is a kind of rest.
