Udān

Udān means flight — the act of rising. In Hindi, it carries the sense of a dream taking wing.

Write down what you want to do, become, or try. It can be huge (“travel the world”) or small (“wake up early for a week”). Both matter equally.

When you achieve a dream, set it free. Watch it move from the wire to the sky. Over time, your sky fills — and you see just how much you’re capable of.

Your dreams are saved only on this device.
