Dīyā

A diya is a small oil lamp. It does not light up a stadium. It lights up a corner. And sometimes that is enough.

Your inner light does not need to be grand. It might be as simple as the belief that tomorrow will be different. Or the memory of someone who saw the best in you. Or the stubborn refusal to give up.

Name what feeds your flame. And when the wind blows hard, remember — even the smallest diya can outlast the darkness.
