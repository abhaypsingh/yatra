Prashna

Prashna means question. In ancient Indian philosophy, the right question was considered more valuable than any answer.

This is a journal for the questions that keep you up at night. “Why do people change?” “What makes something beautiful?” “Is it possible to truly know someone?”

Some questions you will answer over time. Add your insights as they come. Others will stay open forever — and those are often the most important ones.

Your questions are saved only on this device.
