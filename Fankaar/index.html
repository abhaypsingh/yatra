The artist is an alchemist. They take raw experience — pain, joy, longing, wonder — and transform it into something that changes others. The fankaar does not merely express themselves; they create a doorway through which you can enter and find your own feelings waiting on the other side. Their medium does not matter. What matters is the transformation: raw life into something transmissible.

You have been changed by fankaars. A musician whose melody became your inner soundtrack. A painter whose image rearranged your seeing. A writer whose sentence made you feel understood for the first time. These artists did not know you, yet they reached inside you and moved the furniture.
