Saudā

Life is a hidden economy. Every choice is a trade. You chose this city, so you gave up that one. You chose this partner, so you released that possibility. You chose safety, and adventure walked away. Or you chose adventure, and safety waited at home, wondering when you would return.

The saudas of life are not always conscious. Sometimes you only realise what you traded years later, when you notice what is missing. The question is not whether the bargain was perfect — no bargain is — but whether you understood what you were giving and what you were getting.

Name the bargains. They are the ledger of your life’s hidden exchanges.
