Icchā

A wish is a conversation with the future. It says: this is what I hope for. It says: this matters to me.

Some wishes will come true — mark them granted. Some you will outgrow — mark them released. And some will simply wait, patient as starlight.

Over time, your wishes become a map of who you were becoming.

Your wishes are stored only on this device.
