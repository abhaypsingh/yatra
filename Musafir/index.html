Musāfir

The old Hindi songs got it right. Musafir hoon yaaron — I am a traveller, friends. Not someone who has arrived. Not someone who knows the way. Just someone walking.

You are on journeys that have no guidebook. The journey from needing your parents to being your own person. The journey from following to leading. The journey from not knowing who you are to catching glimpses.

Honour the travel itself. The scenery along the way is as important as the destination. And some journeys are beautiful precisely because they have no end.
