You cannot choose your feelings. They arrive like waves — sometimes gentle, sometimes crashing, always moving. The mistake is thinking you should be able to stop them.

The wave of anger after an unfair moment. The wave of joy when a friend says exactly the right thing. The slow, heavy wave of missing someone. None of these are wrong. They are the ocean inside you, doing what oceans do.

Write what you feel as it moves through you. Not to fix it — just to witness it.
