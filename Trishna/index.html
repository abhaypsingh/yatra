Trishnā

Not all desire has a name. Sometimes you sit by a window and feel a pull toward something you cannot identify. It is not hunger, not loneliness, not boredom — it is something underneath all of those.

This unnamed thirst is telling you something. It is the part of you that knows there is more — more feeling, more meaning, more life — even before your mind can put it into words.

Write what you feel, even if you cannot name it. The words do not have to be perfect. They just have to be honest.
