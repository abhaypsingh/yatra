About Chaaya

Shadow work is the practice of looking at the parts of yourself you reject, hide, or feel ashamed of — and accepting them as part of who you are.

You might hide your sensitivity, your anger, your sadness, your need for attention, your fear of being alone. None of these are wrong. They are human.

When you embrace a shadow, it doesn’t disappear. It becomes something you carry with awareness instead of shame.
