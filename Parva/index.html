Parva

In the Mahabharata, a parva is a book — a chapter in the great epic. Your life is an epic too.

Some chapters begin with a bang: a new school, a new city, a loss. Others begin so quietly you only notice them looking back. A friendship that deepened. A fear you outgrew. A question that changed how you see the world.

Mark the chapters as you live them. One day you will look back and see the shape of your story.
