Pratibimba

the reflection

You know what you look like on the outside. But do you know what you look like on the inside?

You will answer twelve questions. Not quizzes — mirrors.
There are no right answers. Only honest ones.

Your answers are saved. Come back in a month, a year, five years.
Watch who you were become who you are.

The person you are today is worth knowing. Even the parts you have not looked at yet.

Pratibimba प्रतिबिम्ब

Pratibimba (Sanskrit: reflection, mirror image) is the image that appears when consciousness turns inward. Not the face you show the world, but the one that exists when you are honest with yourself.

These twelve questions are not a personality test. They are mirrors. Your answers today will be different from your answers in a year. That is the point. Growth is invisible while it is happening. But if you leave a mark, your future self can see the distance traveled.

All portraits are stored on this device alone. They belong to no one but you.
