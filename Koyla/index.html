People love the diamond story. Pressure creates beauty, they say. What they do not say is that most coal does not become diamond. Most coal just burns or breaks.

The question is not whether you can handle pressure. You can — you already do, every day. The question is whether the pressure you are under is the right kind, applied the right way, with the right support around you.

Pressure from someone who believes in you feels different from pressure applied by someone who just needs results. Name the pressures. Not all of them deserve your endurance.
