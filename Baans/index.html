The strongest trees are not the ones that stand rigid against the wind. They are the ones that sway. Bamboo survives typhoons not because it is hard, but because it knows how to give.

You have this quality too. The friendship that survived a fight because someone was willing to bend. The plan that changed and became something better. The part of you that adapts when life does not go as expected. This is not weakness. This is the deepest kind of strength.

Name what bends in your life. It is holding more than you know.
