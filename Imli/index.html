Imlī

Sourness is not the enemy of sweetness — it is its essential partner. The chaat vendor knows this: without the dark imli chutney, the sweet one has no meaning. Without the pucker, the tongue cannot appreciate the relief. Difficulty is the tamarind of life — the flavour you would never choose on its own but without which everything else tastes flat.

You have tasted imli. The failure that made the later success vivid. The loss that made the next hello ache with gratitude. The hard years that gave the easy ones their depth.

Name the sourness. It is the seasoning that made your life worth tasting.
