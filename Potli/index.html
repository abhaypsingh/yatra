Potlī

The potli is a small cloth bundle tied with a string. In old stories, travellers carried everything they needed in one.

Your potli holds what is precious and what is heavy — often the same things. The secret ambition. The unsaid apology. The fear you have carried so long it feels like a part of you.

Opening the potli does not mean throwing things away. It means looking at what you carry and asking: do I still need this? Does it still serve me?
