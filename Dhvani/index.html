Dhvani ध्वनि

Dhvani (Sanskrit: sound, resonance) is the principle that every sound carries meaning beyond its literal content. In Indian aesthetics, dhvani is the suggested, resonant quality of music — the feeling between the notes.

These sounds are generated live in your browser. No recordings, no downloads, no data sent anywhere. Layer them to create the atmosphere you need — for studying, sleeping, thinking, or simply being.

Your browser becomes your sanctuary.
