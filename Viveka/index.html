Viveka विवेक

Viveka means discernment, discrimination between the real and unreal. In Vedantic philosophy, it is the first and most essential quality on the path to freedom.

In everyday life, viveka is the ability to distinguish between what is truly your responsibility and what has been placed on you by others. Children often carry burdens that were never theirs — a parent's sadness, another person's anger, situations they could not control.

Learning to set those down is not abandonment. It is wisdom.
