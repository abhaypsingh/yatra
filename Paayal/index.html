Pāyal

In old Hindi films, you hear the paayal before you see the heroine. The sound is anticipation made musical. You know someone beautiful is about to appear because the anklet has already told you.

You have paayals too. Your reputation arrives before you do. Your energy enters a room before your body. The way people describe you when you are not there — that is your paayal.

Name the anklets. The sounds you make in the world. The ones you chose and the ones others hear whether you intend them or not.
