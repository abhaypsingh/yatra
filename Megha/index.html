Megha

A megha is a cloud — it comes, it passes, it changes shape. Your thoughts are the same. They feel permanent, but they are not.

Writing a thought down is like watching a cloud from below. You see it clearly for the first time. And then, slowly, it moves on.

Let your thoughts drift here. You do not have to hold them. Just notice them.
