Niti

Niti means policy, principle, a personal rule of conduct. Not the rules adults give you — the rules you give yourself.

“Never make fun of someone for trying.” “If someone eats alone, sit with them.” “Say the hard thing before it gets harder.”

These come from experience. Something happened, and you decided: I will always or I will never. That is your niti. Write it in stone.
