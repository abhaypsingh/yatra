Time Capsules

Samay समय

Samay means time. In Indian philosophy, time is not a line but a river — always flowing, never the same water twice.

This is a time capsule for a single day. Not the big events — the small ones. What you ate. What you wore. What the weather was. The things that seem forgettable are the ones that, a year from now, will bring you to tears with their ordinariness.

Everything stays on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.
