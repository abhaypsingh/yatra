Dhārā

A stream does not stop. It cannot. It moves around rocks, under bridges, through seasons. It is never the same water twice, but it is always the same stream.

Your life has streams too. The friendships that keep evolving. The way your taste in music changes. The conversation with a parent that has been going on your entire life, one sentence at a time. These are not events — they are flows. Ongoing, shifting, alive.

Name the streams. The things that are always moving, always changing, always there.
