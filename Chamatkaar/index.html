Chamatkār

We live in an age that distrusts miracles. Everything must have a cause, a mechanism, a peer-reviewed explanation. And yet — there are events in your life that refuse to be explained. The timing that was too perfect to be coincidence. The survival that defied every medical prediction. The connection that materialized from nowhere exactly when you needed it.

A chamatkaar does not require you to abandon reason. It only asks you to acknowledge that some things happened which your understanding cannot contain. The miracle is not in the supernatural — it is in the gap between what should have been and what was.

Record what defied your expectations. Let the inexplicable remain inexplicable, and honour it anyway.
