Hasya

Hasya is one of the nine rasas — the essential emotion of humor and joy. In classical Indian aesthetics, it is considered sacred.

This is a journal for the things that make you laugh. A friend’s terrible pun. A cat falling off a table. The time you said something so wrong it became legendary.

On hard days, come back here. Read your laughs. Remember: if you can laugh, you can survive anything.

Your laughs are saved only on this device.
