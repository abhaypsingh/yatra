A holiday is not a date on a calendar. It is a feeling. The feeling that right now, nothing is required of you.

You felt it as a child without knowing its name. Lying on the floor drawing. Watching rain from the balcony. The last day of exams when the afternoon stretched like an ocean.

You need these moments. Not as rewards for productivity, but as the breathing spaces that make you human. You are not a machine that runs until it breaks. Name the times you truly rested. They are teaching you what peace feels like.
