Nagmā

The earworm of the soul is not a nuisance — it is a signal. When a melody lodges in your mind and refuses to leave, it is because something in its pattern matches something in your life. The refrain you hum while washing dishes is not random. It is the soundtrack your subconscious has chosen for this chapter. Certain phrases become life-soundtracks because they say what you cannot.

You carry nagme. A lullaby from childhood. A film song from a year that changed you. A line of poetry that became a prayer. These melodies did not ask permission — they moved in and stayed.

Name the melodies. They are the refrains that narrate your inner life.
