Prāṇa प्राण

Prāṇa means breath, life force, vital energy. In yoga, prāṇāyāma — the regulation of breath — is considered the bridge between body and mind.

The four patterns here are drawn from clinical and contemplative traditions. Box breathing is used by the military and first responders. The 4-7-8 pattern was developed for anxiety and sleep. All of them work by activating your parasympathetic nervous system — your body’s built-in calm.
