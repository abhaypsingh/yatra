Pankh

A pankh is a wing. But freedom is rarely about flying. It is about having choices. The more things you know how to do, the freer you become.

Some wings are given — a safe home, a good school. Some you build yourself — a skill practised until it becomes part of you.

Name the things that give you freedom. You will realise you have more wings than you thought.
