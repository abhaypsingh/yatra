Pratham

Every life is made of firsts. The first time you held a pen. The first time you swam without touching the bottom. The first time you felt your heart race and didn’t know why.

Firsts are thresholds. On one side is who you were. On the other is who you became. You cross them once, and there is no going back.

Write them down. You are standing at the edge of so many more.
