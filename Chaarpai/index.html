Chārpāī

The chaarpai is Indian furniture at its most democratic. It costs almost nothing. It can be carried anywhere. It breathes in summer and can be dragged into the sun in winter. On a chaarpai, there is no pretension. You sit or lie as you are.

The deepest conversations of your family probably happened on a chaarpai. Elders talking after dinner. Children falling asleep to the sound of grown-up voices. The creak of rope under shifting weight. Life at its most natural pace.

Name the cots. The places where no one was performing. Where life just happened.
