Noise is not always sound. It is anything that keeps you from hearing what matters.

The noise of other people’s opinions drowning out your own voice. The noise of comparison. The noise of your own inner critic repeating the same doubts.

You cannot silence the world. But you can name the noises. And once named, they lose some of their power. What is the loudest noise in your life right now?
