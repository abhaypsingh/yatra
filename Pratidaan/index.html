Pratidān

Pratidān means a return gift — something given back out of love, not obligation.

You receive so much every day without noticing. This is a practice of giving something back — small things. A compliment you meant. Helping without being asked. Listening when someone needed it. Making someone laugh. Choosing patience when you could have chosen anger.

The gift doesn’t have to be big. It just has to be real.
