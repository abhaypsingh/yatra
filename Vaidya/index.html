Vaidya

The vaidya heals holistically. Before the prescription comes the listening. Before the remedy comes the understanding. The vaidya knows that the body is not separate from the mind, that grief lodges in the chest, that worry tightens the stomach, that loneliness weakens the bones. The cure must reach where the illness actually lives.

Your vaidyas may not have been medical at all. A friend who listened until the pain loosened. A teacher who saw the struggle behind the failure. A place that restored what the city had drained. The listener is the first healer. The prescription is often simply: you are heard.
