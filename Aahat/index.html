Change does not arrive without warning. There are always footsteps — small signs that something is shifting. A friend who starts calling less. A feeling of restlessness that will not go away. A new interest that keeps pulling at you.

Most people ignore the footsteps. They are busy, distracted, or afraid of what is coming. But if you learn to listen — really listen — you can hear the future approaching. Not to stop it, but to be ready.

Write down the footsteps you hear. The signs that something in your life is about to change.
