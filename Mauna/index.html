Being alone is not the same as being lonely. Loneliness is the feeling that no one is choosing you. Solitude is the act of choosing yourself. They sound the same from the outside. From the inside, they are different worlds.

You are allowed to enjoy your own company. You are allowed to need other people. You are allowed to sit in silence and not call it sadness. The world is very loud. Knowing how to be quiet is a superpower.

Mauna मौन

Mauna means silence, the practice of intentional quietness. In many contemplative traditions, mauna is not the absence of something — it is the presence of awareness.

Research on solitude shows that the ability to be comfortable alone is one of the strongest predictors of emotional health. But not all aloneness is equal — chosen solitude nourishes, while forced isolation wounds.

Learning to tell the difference is one of the most important skills you can develop.
