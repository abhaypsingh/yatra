the balcony

A jharokha is the carved balcony window of old Indian homes — a place between inside and outside. You stand there to watch the world without fully entering it. Jharokhā

The jharokha was designed for seeing without being fully seen. For listening to the street while staying in the safety of home.

There is nothing wrong with the threshold. Sometimes you need to watch before you join. Sometimes observation is its own form of participation.

But if you find yourself always on the balcony and never on the street, it is worth asking — what am I afraid will happen if I step down?
