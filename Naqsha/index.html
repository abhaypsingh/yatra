Your map is not the same as anyone else's. The park that feels magical to you might be ordinary to your friend. The street corner where something happened — good or bad — is marked on your map forever, invisible to everyone else.

This is the geography of your inner world. The safe places, the scary places, the places where important things happened. Drawing this map is a way of understanding where you have been and where you feel at home.
