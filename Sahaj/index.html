Sahaj means natural, effortless, innate. It is the opposite of struggle.

Everyone has things that flow through them without friction — making people laugh, sensing when someone is upset, drawing without thinking, finding the rhythm in things, remembering song lyrics after hearing them once.

These are not achievements. They are not on any test. But they are your gifts. Name them, so you can see them.
