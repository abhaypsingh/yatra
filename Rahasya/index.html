Rahasya

Rahasya means secret — not the kind you keep from others to deceive, but the kind you keep because the truth is still too raw, too fragile, too yours.

“I miss who I used to be.” “I’m afraid I’m not enough.” “I love someone who doesn’t know.”

Writing a secret down does not betray it. It gives it a safe place to breathe. And sometimes, just writing it is enough to set it free.
