Darpaṇ दर्पण

Darpaṇ (Sanskrit: mirror) is not just a surface that reflects — it is the act of seeing yourself clearly. In Indian philosophy, true seeing requires both honesty and compassion.

Each day you check in, you add a mark to your mirror. Over weeks and months, patterns emerge that you cannot see from inside a single day. The sad weeks pass. The peaceful days cluster. The anxious stretches teach you what triggers them.

Your entries stay on your device. This mirror belongs to you alone.
