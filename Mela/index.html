Melā

A mela is magnificent and exhausting in equal measure. You cannot see everything, do everything, be everywhere. And that is the point.

Growing up is like walking through a fair that never closes. New attractions appear every year. Friends pull you toward one stall while your parents point to another. The noise is incredible. The colours are blinding. And sometimes, in the middle of all of it, you just want to sit on a bench and breathe.

That is okay. The fair will still be there when you stand up. Name the chaos. Name the wonder. They are the same thing.
