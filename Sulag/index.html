Sulag

A smoulder is more dangerous than a flame. A flame is visible, dramatic, easily extinguished. A smoulder hides. It eats through wood from the inside. By the time you see the damage, the structure is already hollow.

But not all smoulders are destructive. The slow burn of determination. The quiet fire of a dream you refuse to abandon. The steady warmth of love that does not need grand gestures. Some smoulders keep you alive through the coldest nights.

Name the smoulders. The destructive ones need air — speak them, release them. The nourishing ones need fuel — feed them, protect them.
