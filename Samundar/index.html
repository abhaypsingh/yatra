Samundar

They will tell you to control your emotions. To keep them manageable, measured, small. But some feelings are not small. Some feelings are oceanic.

The grief of losing a grandparent who was your whole world. The love you feel that has no words in any language. The loneliness of being surrounded by people and still feeling unseen.

You do not need to drain the ocean. You need to learn to swim in it. Name the vastness. You are not drowning — you are discovering how deep you go.
