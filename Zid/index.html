Stubbornness and determination are the same thing, seen from different angles. When you refuse to give up on a friend, they call it loyalty. When you refuse to give up on yourself, they call it courage.

Sometimes your stubbornness will cost you. You will hold on to something too long, or fight a battle that was not worth fighting. But even then, the refusal to surrender is a kind of fire. It means you care enough to burn.

Name the times you refused to let go. The beautiful stubbornness and the costly kind. They are both part of who you are.
