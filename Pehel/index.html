The world moves because someone goes first. Every friendship started because one person said hello. Every change started because one person said this is not right. Initiative is not about being confident.

It is about moving before you feel ready. The hand you raise in class when you are not sure of the answer. The door you knock on when you do not know who will answer. Going first is always a risk. But it is also always a beginning.

Name the times you went first. They are the moments that prove you are braver than you think.
