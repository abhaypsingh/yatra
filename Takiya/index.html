It knows about the tears you will not show anyone. The prayer you whisper even though you are not sure you believe. The replaying of a conversation where you wish you had said something different. The smile you allow yourself when you remember something good.

These private moments are not weakness. They are where your real life happens. Name what the pillow knows. It has been your most faithful confidante.
