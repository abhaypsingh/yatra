Tārā

your sky

Every experience you enter becomes a star in your sky. Some skies are full. Some have just begun. Both are beautiful.

Your sky is not a test. It is a map of where you have been and where you might go.

Come back whenever you want to see how far you have traveled.

Tārā तारा

Tārā (Sanskrit: star) means both “star” and “she who carries across.” In Hindu mythology, Tara is the goddess who guides travelers through the darkness.

Your sky tracks every experience you visit. Each lit star is a step in your journey. The constellations form around the themes of your exploration — inner work, healing, growing, seeing others.

There is no goal. There is no order. Your sky fills at its own pace, in its own pattern. That pattern is yours alone.
