Gehrāī

Gehrai means depth. Some questions are not meant to be answered. They are meant to be lived with, turned over, carried.

The questions you ask say more about you than the answers you find. A person who asks “why do people suffer?” is already someone who cares.

Write the questions that keep you up at night. You do not need answers. You just need the courage to ask.
