Āwāz

Your inner voice is the most constant companion you will ever have. It is the first thing you hear in the morning and the last thing at night.

Sometimes it is kind. Sometimes it is cruel. The first step to changing it is noticing what it says.

Write down what your inner voice tells you. Over time, you will see its patterns — and you can choose which ones to keep.
