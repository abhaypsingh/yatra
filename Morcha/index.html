Morchā

Standing your ground is terrifying. Your voice shakes. Your hands tremble. Everyone is watching, and most of them want you to sit down.

But there are moments when sitting down is not an option. When the cost of silence is higher than the cost of speaking. These moments define you more than any achievement on your CV.

And the stands you did not take — those matter too. They teach you what you were not yet ready for. Name them all. The brave ones and the ones you wish you could redo.
