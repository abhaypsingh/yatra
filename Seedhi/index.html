Sīḍhī

Nobody learns courage in a single day. Nobody masters kindness in one conversation. The important things in life are staircases — you climb them slowly, and sometimes you slip back a step or two.

The staircase is not a failure. It is the design. Some understanding can only arrive gradually, through repetition and patience and the willingness to keep climbing even when you cannot see the top.

Name what you are learning. Honour the step you are on, even if it is not the last one.
