A diamond has many facets. Turn it and the light catches a different face each time. You are the same. The person you are with your best friend is not the person you are with a stranger. Neither is more real than the other.

Growing up means discovering new facets of yourself — some you are proud of, some that surprise you, some that scare you a little. That is not inconsistency. That is depth.

Name the sides of yourself you have noticed. All of them belong to you.
