Dhund

Fog is not darkness. In darkness, you know something is missing. In fog, the world is still there — you just cannot see it clearly. Everything is shapes and guesses and trust.

Some of the most important stretches of life happen in fog. You do not know what you want. You do not know who you are becoming. You cannot see the next step until you take it. And yet — you keep walking.

Write about your fog. Not to clear it, but to honour the courage it takes to move through it.
