The most powerful stories are not in books. They are the ones you whisper to yourself every day. I am not brave enough. I am not smart enough. I am the one people forget. These stories feel like facts.

But they are not facts. They are tales. And tales can be rewritten. The story you told at eight does not have to be the story you tell at eighteen. You are the author. You can cross out a line and write a new one whenever you are ready.

Name your tales. The stories you live inside. Then ask: is this the story I want to keep telling?
