Smriti स्मृति

Smriti (Sanskrit: memory, remembrance) refers to knowledge carried forward through time — the wisdom that is remembered and passed from one generation to the next, from one version of yourself to the next.

You are not the same person you were a year ago. You will not be the same person a year from now. Growth is invisible while it's happening. But if you leave a mark — a letter, a thought, a feeling — your future self can look back and see how far they've come.

These letters are stored only on this device. They cannot be read by anyone else. They are sealed until the date you choose. They are yours alone — a conversation between who you are and who you will become.
