Chhāp

You are not made only of yourself. You are made of every person who ever touched your life — their words pressed into you like a seal into warm wax.

Some imprints are gentle: the way your mother hums while cooking. Some are sharp: the one sentence a teacher said that rearranged everything. Some come from people you barely knew.

Write them down. The people who shaped you deserve to be named, even if they never know it.

Your imprints are stored only on this device.
