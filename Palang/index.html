Palang

Insomnia is a form of honesty. The 3am self is the one who cannot lie, cannot distract, cannot scroll away from the truth. Lying on a palang in the dark, staring at the ceiling, you meet the person you actually are — not the one you perform during the day. The cot is a confessional. The darkness is the priest.

You have had those hours. The night before a decision. The night after a loss. The night when something you had been avoiding finally sat on the edge of your bed and refused to leave. Those were the hours when your real thinking happened — not in meetings, not in conversations, but alone on a palang in the dark.

Name those hours. They are the moments when you were most truthfully yourself.
