We are told to put away childish things. But the things of childhood are not always childish.

The stuffed animal that taught you comfort. The game that taught you fairness. The story that taught you bravery. These were not toys — they were teachers.

You can outgrow something and still honour it. You can be growing up and still keep a piece of the child who got you here. Name what you loved. It shaped you more than you know.
