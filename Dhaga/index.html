Dhāgā

A thread is almost nothing. You could snap it with two fingers. But a thousand threads woven together can hold anything. That is how relationships work — not through one big thing, but through ten thousand small ones.

The inside joke. The goodnight text. Remembering that your friend does not like loud places. Saving the last piece for your sibling. These threads are invisible to everyone else, but they are the strongest material in the world.

Name your threads. The tiny habits of love that hold your people close.
