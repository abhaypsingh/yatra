The hardest part of choosing is not picking the right option. It is grieving the one you left behind.

Every choice closes a door. Sometimes gently, sometimes forever. You chose to tell the truth and lost a friendship. You chose safety and lost an adventure. You chose to stay and lost the chance to go.

This is not failure. This is the price of being human in a world that offers more paths than one life can walk. Name the choices. Honour the sacrifice hidden inside each one.
