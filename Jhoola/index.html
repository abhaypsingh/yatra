Jhūlā

Oscillation is the natural rhythm of a well-lived life. The swing does not stay at the top or the bottom — it moves. The person who never swings never feels the wind. The person who tries to stop the motion only falls. The wisdom is in the surrender to the arc — knowing that the terror at the peak is the price of the joy on the way down.

You have swung. Between hope and despair, between certainty and doubt, between holding on and letting go. Each arc carved something in you that stillness never could.
