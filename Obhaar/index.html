Ubhār

A swell in the ocean is invisible from shore. It moves through deep water, gathering mass and momentum, and only when it reaches the shallows does it rise into a wave that everyone can see. The swell was always there — the wave is just its announcement.

Your life has had swells. A dissatisfaction that grew quietly for years before you finally left. A talent that practised in private before it performed in public. A grief that you held underwater until it refused to stay down. These risings were not sudden — they only appeared sudden to those who were not paying attention.

Name the swells. Honour the invisible force that preceded every visible change.
