Your generation lives in the most dazzling time in human history. Everything shines — screens, filters, success stories, influencer lives. The shine is engineered to attract you.

But the people who changed the world were rarely shiny. They were persistent, kind, stubborn, deeply ordinary in their habits and extraordinary in their commitment.

Learn to love the glow more than the shine. The glow is quieter, steadier, and real. Name what dazzles you. Then look for what glows.
