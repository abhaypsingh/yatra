Nidrā निद्रा

Nidrā means sleep, rest, the dissolution of waking consciousness. In yoga philosophy, yoga nidrā is the practice of conscious release — letting go of what the day accumulated so the deeper self can breathe.

This is not meditation. This is simpler. Name what you carry. Watch it leave. Plant one intention. Sleep.

Everything stays on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere. This is yours alone.
