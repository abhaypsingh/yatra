About Sutra

Sutra means “thread” in Sanskrit. The oldest wisdom traditions were passed down as sutras — short threads of truth strung together.

This is your personal thread. A sentence from a book that shook you. Something a friend said that you never forgot. A lyric that felt written for you. An idea that arrived in the shower.

Over time, your thread becomes a map of what you believe. Everything is saved only on this device.
